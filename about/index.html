about

i'm siddhi/sidharth/sidharthify, i occasionally ship roms, write bash scripts and live in the UNIX terminal. i’m maintaining devices for roms like yaap and cipheros, learning some cpp, and running nixos with a small homelab.

i like rock, as you may have already guessed. my favorite band is Pink Floyd. i also like IDM/EDM and any sort of experimental music as well as some j-pop.

i'm a huge advocate for open-source. i believe Linux is one of the most important projects in the history of computers and that we should focus on building free software for everyone.

i really like AOSP, i maintain ROMs and study AOSP almost every week.

i also seem to be a lot into philosophy. i like reasoning and thinking just for the fun of it. i like thinking about morality, consciousness, life, the universe and religion.

i love computers. i had always been into them ever since i was a kid. i was fascinated by operating systems, custom ROMs, kernels and low level code.

i believe kindness and empathy are some of the best traits a human being can have, along with self respect and dignity.
