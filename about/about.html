about me

Hi there! I'm Sidharth Sharma, an Indian 'developer' & an open-source enthusiast. I like to play with AOSP, low level code and Linux.

  • I maintain YAAP (unofficially for the Nothing Phone 2a and 2a Plus) and CipherOS officially for the Pixel 7 and 7 Pro.
  • I've helped boot AOSP on the Nothing Phone (2A).
  • I have contributed to LineageOS.
  • I run NixOS on my main system.
  • I run a self hosted Plex server with Radarr, Sonarr and Prowlarr.

Besides AOSP development, I love music and anime. Hit me up anytime. I'll try my best to help :3
